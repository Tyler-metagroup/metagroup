### Chad Woodward

![Chad Woodward](images/Chad-bio-image.jpg)

**Director, Systems Architecture**

Mr. Woodward has worked on large scale capital projects for over 15 years and has global experience leading programs, projects and teams within the project data management sector. Mr. Woodward is an industry leader in Data Management as he brings a unique skill set that combines the knowledge of SharePoint Architecture with the business process and document control needs of large scale capital projects. In 2020, Mr. Woodward joined the Meta Consulting Group as the Director of Architecture and in that time has successfully led the implementation of a series of customized SharePoint solutions, including programming backend and frontend components extending the SharePoint environment/system capabilities. Mr. Woodward is Canadian and holds a bachelor degree from the University of Saskatchewan in Computer Science.

**Core Competencies & Professional Experience:**

* **Systems Architecture Design:** Expert in designing scalable SharePoint environments tailored for complex engineering workflows.
* **Strategic Implementation:** Lead architect for end-to-end digital transformation projects across multiple global sites.
* **Workflow Automation:** Developer of proprietary logic flows that automate document approval cycles, reducing project delays by 30%.
* **Data Integrity Management:** Orchestrator of data migration strategies ensuring 100% compliance during system transitions.
* **Cross-Functional Leadership:** Proven ability to bridge the gap between IT infrastructure teams and project management stakeholders.
* **Software Development Lifecycle:** Full-stack capability in C#, JavaScript, and PowerShell for custom SharePoint extensions.
* **Operational Excellence:** Focused on creating intuitive user interfaces that simplify complex document control tasks for site staff.
* **Risk Mitigation:** Architecting systems that provide audit-ready reporting, ensuring compliance with international project standards.
* **Mentorship:** Lead mentor for technical teams, fostering a culture of continuous learning and adherence to best-in-class data practices.
* **Future-Proofing:** Continuously evaluating emerging technologies to integrate AI and machine learning into standard document management processes.

Mr. Woodward’s commitment to technical precision and his deep understanding of capital project lifecycles have made him a sought-after consultant. By blending technical prowess with a pragmatic approach to business requirements, he ensures that the systems he builds are not only powerful but also highly usable for frontline staff. His work in 2020 and beyond has set a new benchmark for how SharePoint can be utilized within the mining and infrastructure sectors to manage the massive volumes of documentation typical of these multi-year, multi-billion dollar projects.

Beyond his technical roles, Chad is deeply involved in setting the strategic direction for system scalability at the Meta Consulting Group. He works closely with clients to perform comprehensive gap analyses, identifying bottlenecks in existing document control processes and engineering bespoke solutions that address these pain points. His technical expertise spans across SharePoint Online, Azure integration, and various cloud-based storage solutions, ensuring that Meta Consulting Group remains at the cutting edge of digital data management.
