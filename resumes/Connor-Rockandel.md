### Connor Rockandel

![Connor Rockandel](images/Connor-bio-image.jpg)

**Managing Director**

Mr. Rockandel has 18 years of hands-on Project Service Experience for large scale engineering and construction projects in the Mineral Resource, Power, Energy & Infrastructure industry. Highly skilled in project setup and execution to meet cost, schedule, and data management targets across all project phases. Mr. Rockandel is experienced in Owner / Operator, Consultant, EPC and EPCM organizations and has extensive experience at various remote sites. Mr. Rockandel jointly founded the Meta Consulting Group with the purpose to help organizations effectively meet document and data management delivery goals regardless of the project's stage or scale. Mr. Rockandel is a Canadian and holds a bachelor of Business Administration from the University of the Fraser Valley and a Masters in Business Administration (honours) from the New York Institute of Technology.

**Core Competencies & Professional Experience:**

* **Project Governance:** Expert in establishing governance frameworks that clarify roles, responsibilities, and accountability for project teams.
* **Strategic Planning:** Skilled in long-term project planning, focusing on resource allocation, budget management, and milestone tracking.
* **EPC/EPCM Expertise:** Deep understanding of the engineering, procurement, and construction management workflows, having operated within all three capacities.
* **Stakeholder Engagement:** Facilitator of high-level communication between executive leadership and field operations teams.
* **Cost Control:** Implementation of rigorous financial tracking systems to ensure projects remain under budget and on schedule.
* **Change Management:** Specialized in helping organizations transition from legacy document systems to modern, cloud-based data management platforms.
* **Operational Resilience:** Building systems that remain robust and functional even under the challenging conditions of remote mining and energy sites.
* **Business Development:** Active in growing the Meta Consulting Group’s footprint, fostering long-term relationships with global industry leaders.
* **Technical Advisory:** Serving as a primary consultant for executive boards on matters concerning document control best practices and industry trends.
* **Academic Foundation:** Leveraging a blend of practical field experience and high-level business strategy to provide actionable advice.

Mr. Rockandel's tenure in the industry has seen him navigate the complexities of remote site operations, where the stakes for data accuracy are highest. His founding vision for the Meta Consulting Group was driven by the realization that many organizations lacked a centralized system to manage the deluge of documentation generated by modern projects. By providing a "one-stop-shop" for data management, he has helped countless clients lower their risk profile and improve their operational efficiency.

His approach is highly collaborative, emphasizing the importance of aligning technical solutions with overarching business objectives. Whether he is leading a team on a new mining project or providing strategic consulting for an energy firm, Connor brings a focus on sustainable growth and process standardization. His educational background, paired with his extensive field experience, allows him to view project challenges through both a logistical and a financial lens, providing a comprehensive strategy for every client he serves. He remains a pillar of the Meta Consulting Group, overseeing the company's expansion into new sectors and reinforcing the commitment to excellence that defines their service delivery.
